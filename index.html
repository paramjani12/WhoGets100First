❓ How to Play

➡ A player rolls the dice. It's score gets added up in current score until dice gets 1 or player hits hold button.
➡ If dice gets 1, player looses current score and other player gets the chance to roll the dice.
➡ If the player hits hold button then the current score get added up to final score and other player gets the chance to roll the dice.
➡ Whichever player reaches the final score of 100 first wins "Who Gets 100 First!!!"
